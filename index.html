featured beaches

St. Regis Maldives

Where luxury meets the ocean — escape to the iconic St. Regis Maldives. Overwater elegance, world-class service, and sunsets you’ll never forget. Indulge in barefoot luxury at the St. Regis Maldives — your island dream awaits.

Villa Nautica

​Villa Nautica Maldives is a chic, nautical-inspired resort just 20 minutes by speedboat from Malé, offering overwater and beachfront villas, gourmet dining, and vibrant marine adventures. With its blend of laid-back island life and cosmopolitan flair, it's an ideal escape for both couples and families.

Kandima Resort

​Kandima Maldives is a vibrant island resort in Dhaalu Atoll, offering a playful blend of luxury, style, and endless activities. It's a tropical playground for couples, families, and friends alike

Lagoon View

A Lagoon View typically refers to accommodations or locations with beautiful views of a tranquil lagoon, offering stunning vistas of crystal-clear waters, lush greenery, and serene surroundings. The views are often found in premium beachfront or overwater villas in resorts across the Maldives.

Furaveri Resort

Furaveri Maldives is a luxurious island resort in Raa Atoll, offering a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. With stunning overwater and beachfront villas, vibrant marine life, and world-class dining, it’s the ideal escape for those seeking both tranquility and excitement in paradise.

fuvahmulah, maldives

​Fuvahmulah is a unique gem in the Maldives, offering a blend of natural beauty, rich biodiversity, and authentic cultural experiences.
